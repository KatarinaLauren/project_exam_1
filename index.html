A SpaceX Timeline

1971

Elon Musk is born

28 June 1971 Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa.

  • Became a multi millionaire in his late 20s
  • 1999 Founder of X.com - later PayPal
  • 2002 Founder and CEO of SpaceX
  • 2003 Co-founder and CEO of Tesla Motors
